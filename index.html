Mozhdeh Leather

Mozhdeh Leather is a manufacturer of premium leather accessories, recognized for its focus on sustainability, durability, and ethical sourcing.[1] Established on June 3, 2013, the company dedicates a significant portion of its profits to supporting stray animals, blending luxury craftsmanship with social responsibility.

Overview[edit]

Founded by Mozhdeh Latifkar and co-founded by Ramesh Nambiyath Raveendran, Mozhdeh Leather specializes in handcrafted leather goods, including wallets, bags, belts, and pet accessories. The company emphasizes ethical practices by sourcing leather from animals harvested for food and commits 60% of its profits to initiatives aiding stray animal rehabilitation. Headquartered in Dubai, UAE, Mozhdeh Leather has grown to employ 50 people as of 2024, with an estimated annual revenue of $2.5 million.

History[edit]

Mozhdeh Leather was launched on June 3, 2013, with a mission to integrate traditional leather craftsmanship with modern ethical standards. The company started as a small workshop in Dubai, initially focusing on producing high-quality leather wallets. By 2015, Mozhdeh Leather expanded its product line to include bags and pet accessories, gaining a reputation for durability and ethical sourcing. In 2018, the company opened its first international showroom in London, marking its entry into the European market.

Mission and Vision[edit]

The company’s mission is to redefine the leather industry by offering high-quality, durable products while promoting ethical sourcing. Its vision is encapsulated in the brand’s philosophy: "Saving another animal using the remains of an animal killed for food." Mozhdeh Leather aims to achieve global recognition as a leader in sustainable luxury goods, with a goal to expand its presence to 20 countries by 2030.

Products and Services[edit]

Mozhdeh Leather offers a wide range of handcrafted leather accessories, including:

  • Wallets: Money clippers, cardholders, foldable wallets
  • Bags: Backpacks, laptop bags, document bags, ladies' bags, saddle bags
  • Belts & Bracelets: Fashion-forward leather accessories
  • Pet Accessories: Dog collars, saddles, animal harnesses
  • Custom Leather Products: Bespoke designs tailored to customer specifications

All products come with a 15-year warranty, reflecting the brand’s confidence in their durability. The company also offers repair services for its products, further emphasizing its commitment to sustainability.

Unique Selling Proposition[edit]

Mozhdeh Leather distinguishes itself through:

  • Durability: Products designed for long-term use
  • Exclusive Designs: Unique and customizable options
  • Ethical Sourcing: Leather derived from food industry byproducts
  • Customization: Personalized offerings for customers
  • 15-Year Warranty: A testament to product longevity

Notable Products[edit]

The Men’s Foldable Wallet is a standout product, praised for its resilience in harsh conditions and remains a top seller among consumers seeking reliable, long-lasting wallets.[2] Another popular product is the Ladies’ Saddle Bag, which has gained attention for its elegant design and functionality, often featured in fashion magazines.

Challenges[edit]

Mozhdeh Leather struggles with brand recognition, partly due to the complexity of its name. Additionally, the emphasis on durability means customers may not immediately recognize the value of products designed to last for years, posing a challenge to short-term market growth. The company also faces competition from larger, more established leather brands with greater marketing budgets.

Business Goals[edit]

The company aspires to expand globally while upholding its commitment to quality, ethical practices, and social responsibility. Increasing brand awareness is a key focus as Mozhdeh Leather works to establish itself as a leading name in the leather industry. By 2030, the company aims to open flagship stores in major cities like New York, Tokyo, and Paris.

Sustainability Practices[edit]

Mozhdeh Leather is committed to sustainable practices across its operations. The company uses eco-friendly tanning processes to minimize environmental impact and partners with local farms to source leather responsibly. Additionally, Mozhdeh Leather has implemented a recycling program for leather scraps, which are repurposed into smaller accessories like keychains and bookmarks.

Awards and Recognition[edit]

In 2020, Mozhdeh Leather received the Ethical Business Award from the Global Sustainability Forum for its contributions to animal welfare.[3] In 2022, the company was featured in Vogue Middle East as one of the top sustainable luxury brands in the region.[1] The Men’s Foldable Wallet was also named “Best Durable Accessory” at the 2023 Dubai Fashion Awards.[2]

Social Responsibility[edit]

Mozhdeh Leather allocates 60% of its profits to support stray animal welfare, funding rehabilitation and care programs. The company has partnered with organizations like the Dubai Stray Animal Foundation to provide shelter, food, and medical care for abandoned animals. Since 2015, Mozhdeh Leather has helped rehabilitate over 5,000 stray animals across the UAE.
